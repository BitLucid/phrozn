Community

Most important part of any Open Source project is an active community.
On this page you'll find ways to get into touch with other Phrozn users and contributors.

IRC Channel

If you have any questions or suggestions, both on using and extending Phrozn, join us at #phrozn channel on freenode.net. Generally, IRC channel is your best bet when you seek quick answer/solution.

Issue Tracker

To report problem, or suggest feature, please, use issue tracker on github.

Twitter Account

Project account: @PhroznProject

You can always contact me via my personal twitter account (@Pawka) as well.

Contribute to Phrozn

Contributing to Phrozn is very easy - just fork the project on github, work on your copy and send pull requests.
If you need assistance on comprehending Phrozn internals, just send me a message using twitter or IRC.
